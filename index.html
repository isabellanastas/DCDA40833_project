The Look of Health

How Visual Culture on TikTok and Instagram Shapes Our Perception of Wellness

An investigation into the aesthetic codes and visual trends that define "healthy" lifestyles on social media.

About This Research

Social media has become the primary source through which young people encounter and internalize ideas about health and wellness.

This research explores the visual language used to communicate health and wellness on TikTok and Instagram.

Key Findings

01

Aesthetic Presentation Influences Perceived Health

Aesthetic presentation strongly shapes health perception, with 100% of respondents choosing the more visually polished option as healthier.

02

People Prefer What Looks Better

People are more likely to choose what looks better, as 93.5% said they would prefer to eat the more aesthetic option.

03

“Healthy” Is Often Defined by Visual Cues

Visual appeal often defines what people consider healthy, with 80.6% saying they chose their preferred option simply because it looked better.

04

Social Media Strongly Shapes Food and Wellness Perception

Social media plays a major role in shaping food and wellness decisions, with 100% of respondents saying they use it for recommendations.
