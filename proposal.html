Project Proposal

Research Focus, Objectives & Methodology

Research Question

How do visual trends on platforms like TikTok and Instagram shape perceptions of what is "healthy" in food and wellness?

This research explores the connection between digital aesthetics and real-world lifestyle choices, examining how the visual presentation of food and wellness routines influences consumer behavior and perceptions of health.

Project Objectives

This project aims to:

  • Identify visual trends in food and wellness content on TikTok and Instagram
  • Analyze how aesthetic presentation influences engagement and perceived value
  • Understand how consumers' choices are shaped by digital aesthetics
  • Highlight the strategic use of visual culture in food and wellness branding

Hypothesis

People are more likely to choose foods, cafes, and wellness routines that look aesthetic online because they associate that look with being healthier or better quality. The visual presentation of food and wellness content on social media directly influences consumer purchasing decisions and lifestyle choices.

Research Methodology

To explore this topic, I will employ a mixed-methods approach combining content analysis and survey research:

  • Content Analysis: Analyze food and wellness content from TikTok and Instagram, focusing on visual trends such as matcha/coffee drinks, acai bowls, and "it girl" routines
  • Comparative Analysis: Compare aesthetic versus non-aesthetic content to observe differences in engagement metrics and audience responses
  • Survey Research: Conduct a survey to understand how aesthetics influence people's purchasing and lifestyle decisions
  • Personal Perspective: Draw from personal experiences creating food-related content and working in wellness environments to support analysis with first-hand insights
  • Visual Trend Identification: Track and document recurring aesthetic patterns and their evolution over time

Project Purpose

The purpose of this project is to better understand how digital aesthetics shape everyday lifestyle choices, particularly in food and wellness. By examining the visual patterns on TikTok and Instagram, this research aims to:

  • Highlight how social media aesthetics influence real-world perceptions of health and wellness
  • Reveal how brands strategically use visual culture to attract consumers
  • Demonstrate the power of digital aesthetics in shaping consumer behavior
  • Provide insight into the relationship between online presentation and purchasing decisions

Target Audience

This project is intended for:

  • Students: Those interested in digital culture, media studies, or consumer behavior
  • Marketers & Brands: Professionals interested in understanding how to leverage aesthetics in food and wellness marketing
  • Social Media Users: Individuals who actively engage with wellness and food content online and want to understand how their choices are influenced
  • Academics: Scholars studying advertising, branding, visual culture, and consumer trends

It is especially relevant for those interested in advertising, branding, and consumer trends, as well as individuals who actively engage with wellness content online.

Exigency & Significance

This topic is important because social media continues to play a major role in shaping how people define health and lifestyle. As platforms like TikTok and Instagram grow, aesthetic trends increasingly influence what people eat, how they exercise, and how they present themselves online.

Understanding this impact is important for:

  • Consumers: To develop critical awareness of how visual culture influences their choices
  • Brands: To understand ethical practices in marketing health and wellness products
  • Platform Designers: To consider how algorithmic amplification affects health perception
  • Educators: To teach media literacy and critical consumption of online content

Research Path

Path: Digital Culture

This project situates itself within the broader study of digital culture, examining how online platforms shape contemporary lifestyle choices and consumer behavior. By analyzing visual trends in food and wellness content, this research contributes to conversations about aestheticization, consumer culture, and the influence of social media on everyday life.
